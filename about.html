About Us   

Collins Shoes Company is a new business for trading sneakers, shoes and all sorts of footwear. This company started very small and grew massively due to the quality products we sell, and based on our willingness to do more. Collins Shoes Company was built in 2018 by Sophia and Samuel Collins. It was located in Congo Town not far from the Bluecrest College. The business had to relocate to a bigger area due to the massive expansion. At first we only used to trade physically but now through our website you can stay anywhere and make a purchase. Your order will be delivered at your home free of charge. We hope to expand our business to international level soon.

At Collins Shoes Company we believe there is a better way to do marketing. A more valuable way, less invasive way where customers are earned rather than bought. We are obsessively passionate about it and our mission is to satisfy all our customers.
